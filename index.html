About

Dr. Mohammad Arif Hossain is an Assistant Professor at Middle Tennessee State University. His research broadly focuses on distributed edge intelligence, AI-assisted cybersecurity, Quantum-assisted ML, and AI-native next-generation networks.

Research Areas

  • AI-assisted cybersecurity
  • Distributed machine learning
  • Edge intelligence and IoT systems
  • Generative AI
  • AI-native next-generation networks
