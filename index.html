About

Dr. Hossain received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the New Jersey Institute of Technology. He is currently a faculty member at MTSU and works broadly in distributed edge intelligence, AI-native networking, cybersecurity, and cyber-physical systems.

His recent efforts span secure and efficient distributed learning, AI-assisted network slicing, edge intelligence for IoT, and resilient future wireless systems.

Research Areas

  • AI-enabled cybersecurity and trustworthy distributed intelligence
  • Distributed and federated machine learning over edge/cloud systems
  • Network slicing, mobile edge computing, and 5G/6G optimization
  • Agentic AI, quantum-assisted ML, and IoT-enabled cyber-physical systems
  • Secure, scalable, and energy-efficient wireless networking architectures
